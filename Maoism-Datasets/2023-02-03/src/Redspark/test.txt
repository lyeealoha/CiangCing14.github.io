<h1 class="entry-title">
CPI (Maoist) Cadre Captured During Raid By NIA Team In Bijapur District </h1>
</header>
<div class="below-entry-meta">
<span class="posted-on"><a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/peoples-war/cpi-maoist-cadre-captured-during-raid-by-nia-team-in-bijapur-district/" title="10:39 am" rel="bookmark"><i class="fa fa-calendar-o"></i> <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2023-01-30T10:39:42+00:00">January 30, 2023</time><time class="updated" datetime="2023-01-30T18:42:27+00:00">January 30, 2023</time></a></span>

</span>
<span class="tag-links"><i class="fa fa-tags"></i><a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/bijapur/" rel="tag">Bijapur</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/bijapur-district/" rel="tag">Bijapur District</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/chhattisgarh/" rel="tag">Chhattisgarh</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/cpi-maoist/" rel="tag">CPI (maoist)</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/cpimaoist/" rel="tag">CPI(maoist)</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/india/" rel="tag">India</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/kamla/" rel="tag">Kamla</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/madkam-ungi/" rel="tag">Madkam Ungi</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/national-investigation-agency/" rel="tag">National Investigation Agency</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/naxal/" rel="tag">Naxal</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/naxalites/" rel="tag">naxalites</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/naxals/" rel="tag">naxals</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/nia/" rel="tag">NIA</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/police/" rel="tag">police</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/political-prisonner/" rel="tag">Political Prisonner</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/ppw-in-india/" rel="tag">PPW in India</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/raipur-en/" rel="tag">Raipur</a>, <a href="https://www.redspark.nu/en/tag/raipur-district/" rel="tag">Raipur District</a></span></div>
<div class="entry-content clearfix">
<p><strong>Bijapur District, January 30, 2023: </strong>A cadre of the CPI (Maoist), wanted in a case related to an attack on a police party in 2021 that left 22 personnel dead and 30 others injured, was arrested in Chhattisgarh&#8217;s Bijapur district, an NIA spokesperson said on Monday.</p>
<p>Madkam Ungi alias &#8220;Kamla&#8221;, a resident of Muthamadgu in Bijapur district, was captured during a raid on Sunday, and subsequently produced before the NIA Special Court in Jagdalpur, the spokesperson said.</p>
<p>She was taken into custody by the NIA-Raipur in connection with a case pertaining to the attack by armed CPI (Maoist) cadres on police and security force personnel near Tekalgudiyam village in Bijapur district in 2021 that resulted in death of 22 policemen and injuries to 30 others, the official of the National Investigation Agency (NIA) said.</p>
<p>The case was initially registered by the Tarrem police station in Bijapur district and was later re-registered by the NIA on June 5, 2021, the spokesperson said.</p>
<p>During investigations, the NIA said, an input was received that a wanted woman Maoist was hiding in the Bhopalpatanam area of Bijapur district.</p>
<p>Immediately, an NIA team from Raipur was mobilized and deployed as part of an operation to apprehend her, the agency said.</p>
<p>Further probe in the case is in progress, the spokesperson said.</p>
